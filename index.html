Welcome to the crazy space inside my head!

My background; I grew up in Portland, OR. Way back, before it was cool, it was a fairly quiet big city to grow up in. I spent my teen years exploring downtown's used clothes and record stores. I love Portlands vibe of being both hippy dippy and cutting edge.

What drives me; Everything I do in life is for my two crazy sons. I want them to know that women can be both nurturing and powerful. I got into code so that they could see you can learn something new at any age and pursue new goals and dreams.

Fun facts about me; I am a huge geek for all things geeky. I honestly love the culture of being a geek. It is so cool to find something like a book, tv show, or movie and just send so much love and energy into that item and it's creater.
